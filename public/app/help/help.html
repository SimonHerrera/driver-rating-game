Here are the driving leaders:

ABOUT - Everyone thinks they are good drivers, and everyone thinks that everybody else on the road is a complete idiot. Someone has to be right, this app was made so that you can see what others think about your driving skills.

SCORE - Your score is a combination of several factors. First, if someone posts a negative or positive comment about a maneuver you pulled your score will be adjusted accordingly. Also, for each day that goes by since the first comment was made about your plate you will 5 bonus points.

SEARCH - To search for a plate, just go to the search page and type in the state abbreviation and plate number you are looking for. The results will show the user score and you can scroll through their messages.

LEADER BOARD - To view the Leader board, just click the Leader board button. Once on the page you can filter by City, State or Zip to see what the current top, (or bottom) scores are for your query.

DELETE - You cannot delete messages about your plate, you must own your driving. To delete your user account, simply click on "Help" and then "Account" to navigate to the Delete Account page. Here you can delete your account, click the "Delete Account" button. This will remove the information you entered when you registered.
